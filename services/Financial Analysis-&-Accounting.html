Frequently Asked Questions

What accounting software do you recommend?

We recommend solutions based on your business size, industry, and specific needs. Popular choices include QuickBooks for small businesses, Xero for growing companies, and SAP/Oracle for enterprises. We help you select and implement the right system.

How often should financial statements be prepared?

We recommend monthly financial statements for active monitoring, quarterly for board reporting, and annual audited statements for compliance. The frequency depends on your business complexity, growth rate, and stakeholder requirements.

Can you handle multi-currency accounting?

Yes! We specialize in international financial management including multi-currency accounting, foreign exchange management, and consolidated reporting for businesses operating across Africa, Europe, and beyond.

What's included in financial analysis services?

Our financial analysis includes ratio analysis, trend analysis, variance analysis, benchmarking, cash flow analysis, and profitability assessment. We provide actionable insights with specific recommendations for improvement.

Do you offer fractional CFO services?

Yes! Our fractional CFO services provide strategic financial leadership on a part-time or project basis, giving you executive-level financial expertise without the cost of a full-time CFO.

How do you ensure data security?

We use encrypted cloud-based systems, secure file transfer protocols, and strict access controls. All staff sign confidentiality agreements, and we comply with international data protection standards including GDPR.
