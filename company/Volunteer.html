Volunteer With AEC

Make a difference by sharing your skills and passion. Help us bridge the digital divide and empower communities across Africa and Europe through technology and education.

Why Volunteer With Us?

At AFRIQUEEUROPECONNEXIONVMETC, we believe in the power of giving back. Our volunteer program allows professionals, students, and technology enthusiasts to contribute their skills toward meaningful projects that create lasting impact.

Whether you have a few hours a month or want to commit to a longer-term project, your contribution can help bridge the digital divide, train the next generation of tech professionals, and support businesses in underserved communities.

Benefits of Volunteering

  • Make Real Impact: See the direct results of your contribution in communities and businesses
  • Expand Your Network: Connect with like-minded professionals across Africa and Europe
  • Develop New Skills: Learn through hands-on experience in diverse projects
  • Gain Experience: Add meaningful volunteer work to your portfolio
  • Flexible Commitment: Choose projects that fit your schedule and interests
  • Official Recognition: Receive certificates and recommendations for your contributions

Volunteer Opportunities

We offer various volunteer positions tailored to different skill sets and time commitments. Find the perfect fit for you!

Training & Mentorship

Share your expertise by training individuals or businesses in technology, digital skills, business analysis, or project management.

Time Commitment: 2-4 hours/week
Skills Needed: Teaching, Technical Expertise

Software Development

Contribute to open-source projects, develop tools for NGOs, or help small businesses build their digital presence.

Time Commitment: Flexible
Skills Needed: Programming, Web Development

Business Consulting

Provide pro bono consulting to startups and small businesses on strategy, operations, marketing, or financial management.

Time Commitment: 3-5 hours/week
Skills Needed: Business Analysis, Strategy

Content Creation

Write blog posts, create educational materials, develop case studies, or produce video tutorials for our community.

Time Commitment: 2-3 hours/week
Skills Needed: Writing, Creativity

Design & UX

Help design websites, marketing materials, or user interfaces for community projects and non-profit initiatives.

Time Commitment: Flexible
Skills Needed: Graphic Design, UX/UI

Translation Services

Translate educational content, documentation, or marketing materials between English, French, and other languages.

Time Commitment: 1-3 hours/week
Skills Needed: Multilingual, Attention to Detail
