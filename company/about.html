Our Story

What began in 2017 as a passionate grassroots initiative has evolved into a comprehensive digital transformation consultancy serving businesses across two continents.

Founded by Armel Sandjong Nantchouang, AFRIQUEEUROPECONNEXIONVMETC started with a simple yet powerful vision: to make enterprise-grade technology accessible to businesses of all sizes. Armed with determination and expertise, Armel began delivering technology solutions hand-to-hand and door-to-door, building trust and relationships one client at a time.

Today, we operate exclusively online with a network of trusted partners and top engineers, bringing together the best minds in AI, ERP, CRM, and cloud services. Our journey from local initiative to international consultancy reflects our commitment to growth, innovation, and the success of our clients.
