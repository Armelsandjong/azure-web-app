Case Study #1: Lost Sales Opportunities

Industry: Manufacturing & Distribution | Company Size: 50-200 employees

The Problem

  • Sales team losing track of customer opportunities
  • No centralized customer communication history
  • Duplicate customer records across Excel files
  • Sales pipeline visibility is non-existent
  • Follow-ups missed, deals falling through
  • No reporting on sales performance
  • Customer complaints not tracked

The Solution

Microsoft Dynamics 365 Sales + Power Pages

  • Unified customer database
  • Automated lead tracking & scoring
  • Visual sales pipeline management
  • Automated follow-up reminders
  • Power BI dashboards for insights
  • Mobile app for field sales
  • Customer self-service portal

Case Study #2: Finance Chaos

Industry: Wholesale & Distribution | Company Size: 100+ employees

The Problem

  • Manual invoice processing taking days
  • Payment tracking in multiple spreadsheets
  • No visibility on cash flow
  • VAT calculations done manually (errors!)
  • Accounts receivable aging not tracked
  • Product release delays due to payment confusion
  • Month-end closing takes 2 weeks

The Solution

Microsoft Dynamics 365 Finance + Power Automate

  • Automated invoice generation
  • Real-time payment tracking
  • Automatic VAT calculation
  • Integrated accounts payable/receivable
  • Cash flow forecasting
  • Automated payment reminders
  • One-click financial reports

Finance Process Map - Order to Cash

Step 1: Customer Order Received

Order entered in Dynamics 365 Sales → Auto-creates sales order

↓
Step 2: Invoice Generation

System auto-generates invoice with VAT calculation → Sent to customer via email

↓
Step 3: Payment Processing

Customer pays → Payment recorded in Accounts Receivable → Bank reconciliation automated

↓
Step 4: Payment Verification

System matches payment to invoice → Updates accounts → Triggers warehouse notification

↓
Step 5: Product Release

Warehouse receives automated release order → Product shipped → Customer notified

↓
Step 6: Financial Reporting

All transactions recorded → Real-time P&L, balance sheet, cash flow reports available

📊 Results Achieved:

80%

Faster Invoice Processing

95%

Payment Accuracy

2 Days

Month-End Closing (from 14)

Case Study #3: Inventory Nightmare

Industry: Retail & E-commerce | Company Size: 20-50 employees

The Problem

  • No real-time stock visibility
  • Overselling products (stock already sold)
  • Understocking (lost sales)
  • No demand forecasting
  • Manual stock counts (time-consuming)
  • Multiple warehouses with no sync
  • Expired products not tracked

The Solution

Dynamics 365 Supply Chain + AI Insights

  • Real-time inventory tracking
  • Multi-warehouse synchronization
  • AI-powered demand forecasting
  • Automated reorder points
  • Product expiry alerts
  • Mobile barcode scanning
  • Predictive analytics for trends

Inventory Management Process Map

Step 1: Goods Receipt

Products arrive → Scan barcode → Auto-update inventory in real-time

↓
Step 2: Stock Allocation

System allocates stock across warehouses → Updates all channels (website, POS, marketplace)

↓
Step 3: Demand Forecasting (AI)

AI analyzes sales history, seasonality, trends → Predicts future demand

↓
Step 4: Automated Reorder Alerts

When stock reaches reorder point → Auto-generates purchase order → Notifies supplier

↓
Step 5: Order Fulfillment

Customer orders → System picks from optimal warehouse → Updates stock → Ships product

↓
Step 6: Stock Analysis

Real-time dashboards → Slow-moving items identified → Expiry alerts → Optimization recommendations

📊 Results Achieved:

60%

Reduction in Stockouts

40%

Reduced Overstock

25%

Improved Cash Flow

How Power Pages Enhances These Solutions

Microsoft Power Pages provides customer-facing portals that extend Dynamics 365 capabilities

Customer Self-Service

Customers can check order status, track shipments, view invoices, and submit support tickets without calling.

Online Ordering

B2B customers place orders directly through portal → Auto-syncs with Dynamics 365 → Eliminates manual entry.

Invoice Portal

Customers view outstanding invoices, download PDFs, make payments online → Reduces payment delays.

Support Tickets

Submit support requests → Auto-creates cases in Dynamics 365 → Track resolution status in real-time.

Partner Portals

Distributors and partners access real-time inventory, place orders, track commissions through secure portal.

Mobile Access

Fully responsive design → Customers and partners manage everything from smartphones.
